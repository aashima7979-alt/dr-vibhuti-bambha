Khana khane ke baad bloating ya heaviness?

FAQs

Common Questions

Kab doctor ke paas jaana chahiye? +

Jab acidity, gas, constipation ya bloating regular ho jaye — tab delay nahi karna chahiye. Early treatment se faster aur better recovery hoti hai.

Kya acidity serious ho sakti hai? +

Haan, agar ignore karein toh long-term damage ho sakta hai — jaise oesophagitis ya ulcers. Early treatment best hai aur zyada asaan bhi.

Online consultation possible hai? +

Haan! Aap Instagram par DM karke online consultation le sakte hain. Most conditions ke liye yeh equally effective hai.

Kya unnecessary tests karwaye jaate hain? +

Bilkul nahi. Dr. Vibhuti ka approach honest hai — sirf wahi tests jo genuinely zaroorat hain. Pehle proper consultation aur clinical assessment hota hai.

Kitne sessions mein result milega? +

Zyada tar patients 2–3 weeks mein significant improvement feel karte hain. Yeh condition ki severity par depend karta hai — jo first consultation mein clearly discuss kiya jaata hai.
